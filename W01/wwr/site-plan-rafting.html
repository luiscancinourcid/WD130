Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to be easier to find for the prospective clients and they can know why this company is the best choice, to has all the information about the company, information of all types of silicone hoses this company manufactures so the prospective clients can know this company, trust and do business with it.

Audience

The kind of people we want to visit this site are companies which operate in the heavy transportation, earthmoving, agricultural, industrial and marine parts, companies which are interested in silicone hoses made with a good quality and low price. The silicone cost inscresed about 300% this year (2022) with the website we are going to give an emphasis of our low prices and also a way to chose the specific silicone hose they are looking for trought their own desktops or laptos. We are going to use orange, yellow and red colors.
